PLUM iTV
SOLUTIONS OVERVIEW

Interactive television is our reality, days of watching live content is far away. Make your subscribers enjoy all the benefits of modern and flexible television.

Plum solution covers complete end-to-end scenario, from provider’s headend to subscribers’ homes.

Plum iTV is offering all popular features and Plum team is constantly developing new functionalities and thus meets the most demanding requires that keep rising every day. The only part that provider is obligated to provide are channels encoded into IP.

Plum is a very open platform that gives a lot of possibilities for integration with other transcoders, STB's and DRM/CAS vendors which makes it suitable for providers who already have some preferences or have already cooperated with some vendors which they prefer.

Plum iTV is solution for IPTV (provider’s managed network, with guaranteed QoS) and for OTT (unmanaged network) too.

PLUM iTV FOR PROVIDERS

With Plum iTV providers can offer full interactivity. Our iTV solution is suitable for Telco providers, Cable providers and Internet providers too. IPTV is used in closed, proprietary, fully-managed networks so provider can completely control Quality of Service. The advantage of our solution is that multicast can also be used for live streaming and like this provider can drastically decrease bandwidth consumption.
