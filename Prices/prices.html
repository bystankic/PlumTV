Partnership Model

Partnership model represents partnership between Headend partner ( cable, TV house, ISP, Telephone company… ) and Plum iTV partner in providing interactive television service ( IPTV / OTT ). Partners join their investment because of which additional money assets necessary for commercial iTV service are minimal or there are none at all.

CLOUD SYSTEM MODEL

Represents renting Plum iTV services, where the service is paid monthly according to the principle “pay as you grow”. Complete Plum iTV platform is on Plum iTV Cloud and Plum iTV service is distributed from cloud to subscribers.

RESELLING MODEL

Reselling model represents partnership between Reselling partner and Plum iTV partner in providing interactive television service ( IPTV / OTT ). Reselling partner resells Plum iTV service to his clients according to the pricelist and under conditions that partners defined together.

PRICE FROM: $___

PERPETUAL LICENSE MODEL

Client permanently buys licenses for using Plum iTV system for providing interactive television service (IPTV/OTT). Client pays all Plum iTV costs that are necessary to build commercial service and thus client becomes independent system owner and has 100% of the revenue.
